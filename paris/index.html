At the landmarks of the Athenian democracy

Step back in time and walk in the footsteps of history... Explore the Ancient Agora of Athens, the vibrant heart of classical Athens, where citizens debated and shaped the first democracy. Stand on the Hill of Pnyx, the birthplace of public speaking and assembly, where great orators like Pericles inspired the ideals of freedom and civic duty. Finally, visit the evocative site of Socrates’ Prison, steeped in philosophical legacy, where the father of Western philosophy spent his final days defending the pursuit of truth.
